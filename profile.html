Dear reader,

From a young age, I was a naturally curious kid, always eager to understand how things worked. I spent hours playing with toys, often breaking them apart to figure out how they were made and what made them function. This curiosity fueled a passion for problem-solving and hands-on learning, which ultimately led me to pursue a deeper interest in technology. That early fascination with mechanics has evolved into a desire to build and create in the tech space, where I can continue exploring how things are built — only now, on a more advanced scale. I’m excited to be part of the fast-paced and ever-evolving world of technology. The constant innovation and change in tech keep me excited to keep learning, adapting, and contributing. I can’t wait to dive deeper into this dynamic industry, where I can help shape the future and create solutions that really make a difference.

In my free time, I love getting out in nature and exploring new places through travel—it helps me stay inspired and refreshed. I also enjoy playing musical instruments, which is a great way for me to relax and get creative. And of course, I’m a big fan of Marvel and sci-fi movies since they spark my interest in futuristic worlds and imaginative stories.

Here’s a glimpse into what drives and defines me:


  • Naturally curious about existence and the nature of how we come to be

  • Intellectual, always seeking deeper understanding

  • Team Player: Thrives in collaborative environments and values teamwork

  • Detail-Oriented: Focused on precision and quality in every project
