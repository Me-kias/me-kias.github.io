Study Choice

Introduction

My name is Mikias Tesfaye, and I am 23 years old. I currently reside in the Netherlands.

Prior Education and Study Background

I finished high school in 2018. Since then, I have taken various online tech courses. These courses have helped me build new skills and stay updated in the tech field. I have participated in various study choice activities. These activities include attending open days, trial study days, and online information sessions.

In addition, I had valuable conversations with a study choice advisor through the HZ platform, which further solidified my decision to pursue the HBO-ICT program.

Motivation for HBO-ICT

My motivation to study HBO-ICT stems from my passion for technology and its endless potential for innovation. From a young age, I have been fascinated by how technology shapes the world and wanted to be part of this transformation.

The HBO-ICT program combines technical skills and practical problem-solving, which perfectly aligns with what I seek. I am eager to deepen my understanding of software development, system design, and information technology, so I can prepare for the ever-evolving demands of the tech industry.

Future Career Goals

After completing the program, I aspire to work as a skilled software engineer, with the ambition to eventually start my own tech business. Over the next four years, I aim to acquire technical expertise and practical experience, alongside developing entrepreneurial skills to help turn my dreams into reality.

Job and Interests

Currently, I do not have a part-time job, but I am considering working about 10-15 hours a week. Aside from my academic pursuits, I enjoy hobbies like photography and music.

In the future, I plan to learn how to play a musical instrument, such as the guitar, allowing me to balance both my creative and technical interests.
