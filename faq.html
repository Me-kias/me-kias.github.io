Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

Go to hz.mynetpay.nl and select "Mobility Print." Choose your current software and install the mobility print app. Select which file(s) you would like to print, choose the "HZ Printer," scan your student card or enter your HZ login details, select "Print release," select your file, enter your preferred settings, and press print!

2. How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

Log into a printer using your HZ student card or username and password. Select the "scan" option.

3. How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

1. Visit the HZ Web Shop: Go to the HZ web shop URL.
2. Find Your Item/Event: Browse or search for the IT introduction event or product.
3. Add to Cart: Select the item/event and add it to your cart.
4. Checkout: Review your cart and proceed to checkout.
5. Enter Details: Fill in your personal and payment information.
6. Confirm Purchase: Review and confirm your purchase.

4. How can you book a project space?

1. Log in to the HZ Portal
2. Navigate to Room Reservations
3. Select the Project Space
4. Review Availability
5. Complete the Booking
6. Access the Space.

5. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

1. Get a Permit: Obtain a parking permit if needed.
2. Find the Lot: Use the campus map to locate the parking areas.
3. Park Properly: Use designated spots and follow parking rules.
